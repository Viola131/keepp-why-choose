Dr.Once
Skincare & Hair Care · Hong Kong SAR
Region
Hong Kong SAR
Focus
Brand Incubation · Strategic OEM · Market Positioning
Categories
Skincare & Hair Care

Dr.Once entered the competitive Hong Kong market with a clear mission: to bridge the gap between clinical treatments and daily self-care. Specializing in high-performance skincare and hair care, the brand aimed to provide "At-Home Aesthetician" results for busy urban professionals facing the environmental stressors of city life.

The Challenge

🔬
Scientific Credibility

Moving beyond "cosmetic" claims to prove "medical-grade" efficacy in both skin and scalp health demanded rigorous substantiation beyond standard certifications.

🎯
Niche Differentiation

Standing out against global giants in the hair-regrowth and anti-aging skincare sectors required a sharply defined brand identity and ownable ingredient story.

⚡
Rapid Scale

Developing a cohesive brand identity across two distinct categories — skincare and hair care — under a single, unified brand voice without diluting either.

The Strategy

1
Patented Formula Integration
We utilized our exclusive library of patented ingredients to create a dual-lane product strategy:
  • Skincare: High-permeability active compounds that mimic clinical injectables, ensuring "water-light" results without the needles.
  • Hair Care: Specialized scalp-recovery formulas using patented bio-active peptides to address thinning and follicle health — a major concern in high-stress markets like Hong Kong.
Proprietary IP Bio-Active Peptides Dual-Category
2
Strategic Brand Positioning
Keepp's marketing team positioned Dr.Once under the concept of "Precision Care." We moved away from generic beauty marketing and instead focused on the "Science of Restoration" — a narrative that appealed to Hong Kong consumers' preference for transparent, results-driven ingredients and professional-looking branding.
Precision Care Science Narrative Local Market Intel
3
Go-To-Market Execution
  • Compliance & Quality: Handled all regulatory requirements to ensure the products were retail-ready for Hong Kong's strict safety standards.
  • Marketing Synergy: Coordinated digital storytelling that educated consumers on the synergy between scalp health and overall skin vitality.
HK Compliance Retail-Ready Digital Storytelling

The Results

🏆
Dual-Category Success

Both skincare and hair care lines launched with a unified premium brand voice resonating across both audiences.

⭐
Authority Status

Recognized by local influencers and skincare experts as a leading "Medical-Grade" home care solution in HK's premium segment.

📈
Accelerated Growth

Rapid market penetration achieved, with a high repeat-customer rate seeking the brand's patented ingredient benefits.

Brightening & Barrier Care

Lumière Lab set out to redefine brightening skincare for Southeast Asian skin tones — moving beyond harsh actives toward a gentler, science-backed approach centered on barrier health and long-term luminosity. Keepp served as their full R&D and go-to-market partner across Singapore and Malaysia.

The Challenge

🌿
Skin Tone Inclusivity

Formulating brightening actives that perform effectively across diverse Southeast Asian skin tones without irritation or over-lightening required precise calibration.

🛡️
Barrier-First Positioning

Differentiating in a crowded brightening category by leading with barrier science rather than traditional vitamin C-only messaging was a significant communications challenge.

🌏
Multi-Market Compliance

Navigating HSA Singapore, Malaysia NPRA, and cross-border ingredient restrictions simultaneously without delaying the launch timeline.

The Results

✨
Clean Launch

Full HSA and NPRA compliance achieved before launch date, enabling simultaneous Singapore and Malaysia retail entry.

🏪
Retail Placement

Secured listings in Sephora SEA within 6 months of launch, driven by the science-forward brand narrative.

📊
Repeat Rate

68% repeat purchase rate in the first quarter, validating the efficacy-led positioning with real consumer behaviour.
